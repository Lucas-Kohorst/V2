What is BTCRIT?

We are a club at RIT focused on Blockchain Technology and other Decentralized systems.

In our weekly meetings we'll discuss news from the Crypto-currency space, projects our members are working on, and occasionally hear from guest speakers.

To see what we've been up to, check out the Meetings page for past meetings or the Club Resume page for our projects.
